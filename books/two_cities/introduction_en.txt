Introduction to the Reader

Welcome to this modernized edition of A Tale of Two Cities by Charles Dickens.

Originally published in 1859, this classic novel is famous for its vivid depiction of the French Revolution, the tragic sacrifice of Sydney Carton, and the timeless themes of love, redemption, and rebirth.

This edition is adapted into clear, modern English. With all due respect to the original work, our goal is to preserve the rich storytelling, character depth, and emotional power of Charles Dickens' masterpiece while offering a more accessible reading flow. It is designed as a helpful tool for language learners, students, and anyone discovering this classic story for the first time.

We hope you enjoy reading this timeless story.
