# Chapter 6 Outline Brainstorm: The 3-Generation Rule

## Core Concept
Ibn Khaldun's famous rule that a dynasty naturally lasts for three generations before it collapses. 
1. **The First Generation:** Retains the tough, desert qualities. Strong *Asabiyyah* (solidarity), shared hardships, bravery, and predator-like hunger.
2. **The Second Generation:** Transitions from nomadic toughness to sedentary luxury. Power becomes centralized in one ruler rather than shared. *Asabiyyah* begins to crack, but the memory of the first generation keeps the empire running.
3. **The Third Generation:** Completely forgets the tough nomadic life. They are consumed by luxury, rely entirely on mercenaries for defense, and are obsessed with titles and status. *Asabiyyah* is completely destroyed. The empire is ripe for conquest by a new hungry group.
