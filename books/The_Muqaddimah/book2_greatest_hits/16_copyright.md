# Copyright & Editorial Notes

### About This Edition

*The Muqaddimah: Essential Passages in Plain English*

The plain-English rendering in this volume is original work. 

The underlying text — *The Muqaddimah* by Ibn Khaldun (1377) — is in the public domain. This edition is not affiliated with, and has not been derived from, any existing English translation, including the Franz Rosenthal translation (Princeton University Press, 1958) or the N.J. Dawood abridgment (1969). Readers seeking a complete scholarly translation are referred to the Rosenthal edition.
