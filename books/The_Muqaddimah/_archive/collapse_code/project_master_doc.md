## 4. The Core Idea That Makes This Sellable
Ibn Khaldun described **asabiyyah** as the bond of cohesion among humans — strongest in the nomadic phase, decreasing as civilization advances. As it declines, another more compelling asabiyyah takes its place; civilizations rise and fall in predictable cycles.
Every civilization follows a predictable cycle: hard struggle -> conquest/dominance -> luxury/vice -> children forget the struggle -> collapse -> new hungry outsiders take over.
