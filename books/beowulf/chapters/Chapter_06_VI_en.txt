A word from within announced:
'To you I am commanded to say by my valorous lord,
the leader of the East Danes, 
           that he knows your noble history,
and you are to him, 
           over sea-swells,
--bold in thought-- 
           welcome hither;
now you may enter 
           in your war-gear,
under visored-helmets,

let battle-boards

Then the mighty one arose, 
           about him many warriors,
the glorious troop of thanes; 
           some waited there,
guarding the gear of war 
           as the hardy leader bade;
they hurried together; 
           the hero led the way for them
under Heorot's roof,
severe under his helmet,
