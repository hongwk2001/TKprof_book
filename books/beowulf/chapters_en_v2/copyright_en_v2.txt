[Editor's Note on the Translation]
This project was driven by a core philosophy: "Easy understanding and fun is more important than literal translation."

We completely discarded stiff literal translations and academic nuances in favor of a rough, explosive, and modern style to maximize the brutality of the battles and the thrill of the action. By treating this epic like a modern fantasy web-novel, we have crafted an "Entertainment Pass" designed to be binge-read. Furthermore, the pacing and sentence structures were specifically tuned to ensure maximum tension during action scenes, making it an incredible experience even when read aloud via Text-to-Speech (TTS) for audiobooks.
