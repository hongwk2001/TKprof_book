About this Project

Classic literature is one of the best ways to acquire new language skills. However, archaic words and complex syntax from the 19th and early 20th centuries often require readers to lookup words constantly, breaking the flow of reading.

Our modernization process systematically updates archaic structures while preserving 100% of the original story details, descriptions, character voices, and emotional depth. It is ideal for shadowing practice, reading comprehension, and active listening.
